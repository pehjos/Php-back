Choosing Z-treebiz as your farming partner

Who we are

Z-treebiz Enterprise is a farming based company found in Langbinsi in North-East Ghana. We have been on the system for two years now. We have over 3500 acres of cashew and still planting. Our company ensures that farmers receive free nursed cashews and later sell it back to us when yielded. Z-treebiz Enterprse also trains farmers on how to raise cashew plants to ensure a good yield.

Why Z-treebiz Enterprise

Z-Treebiz Enterprise gives farmers the best training on how to plant, graft and nurse cashew seedlings to ensure a good start with a good start.

Z-Treebiz Enterprise has a lot of experience as far as the growth of cashew is concerned. Therefore Z-Treebiz Enterprise support the farmers with their experience to ensure a good yield beneficial to both sides of the business.

As part of the contract Z-Treebiz Enterprise does not sell the cashew seedlings to the farmers but rather gives it to them for free.

Z-Treebiz Enterprise does not only go into farming with males but also females. The enterprise has come in to understanding that women also have the potential so therefore giving them the opprtunity to partake in the farming.
